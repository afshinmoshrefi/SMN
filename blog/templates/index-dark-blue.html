Common Questions

78-86% win rate sounds too good to be true. What does that actually mean?

Win rate measures whether a pattern closed higher than its entry. The average holding period is about 20 days. Not every pattern wins by the same amount, and the AI does not control position sizing or stop-losses. The worst individual test year (2025) had a baseline win rate of 61.5% before AI filtering lifted it to 78.1%. Every year in the track record table was scored on data the model had never seen.

What happens when a pick loses?

Not every pick wins. When one misses, the AI Score, Win Probability, and year-by-year historical proof are all visible before you enter. You decide position size and risk management. We give you the edge. You control the risk.

I can see seasonal patterns on TradingView for free. Why pay for TradeWave?

Raw seasonal charts show you what happened. TradeWave's AI scores which patterns are most likely to repeat given today's trend, volatility, and market conditions. That is the gap between historical data and a trading decision. We also provide election-cycle pattern analysis spanning nearly 100 years, which no free tool offers.

Can the patterns stop working?

Market regimes change. That is why the AI retrains and re-evaluates patterns against current conditions every day. During COVID (2020), when most seasonal strategies broke down, the AI actually delivered its strongest performance, lifting risk-adjusted returns by 291%. The model adapts because it scores against live market data, not just historical averages.

What if I subscribe and don't have time to use it?

Every morning before market open, you get one AI pick delivered by email with the ticker, direction, AI Score, and key stats. The entire decision takes under 2 minutes. Most members spend less time on TradeWave than they do checking the weather. And if life gets busy, cancel with one click from your account page. No calls, no retention tricks.

Is this just backtested data?

No. Every year in the track record was scored on data the model had never seen during training, using strict walk-forward validation. The model retrains against current market conditions. It also powers a live proprietary trading strategy. Every pick is published before market open and tracked on our public scorecard. No deleted picks, no revised entries.
